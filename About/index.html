From engineering to real-world impact

Technology and computers have always been a big part of my life. From a young age, I was drawn to understanding how systems work—but for a long time, I wasn’t sure how to turn that passion into something meaningful.

After my military service, I committed fully and studied Software Engineering, including a Master’s degree. I then entered a “classic” high-tech office role. On paper, it was perfect. In reality, it didn’t feel right.

I wanted to build something with clear impact—something I could measure and improve. That’s when I started helping family businesses: building websites, managing Google Ads, and doing organic SEO. Suddenly, everything clicked.

I saw results in real time: rankings rising, calls coming in, and feedback like “our phone won’t stop ringing.”

That direct impact changed everything. I left my job and dedicated myself to mastering marketing at the highest level—because I believe visibility is the core of every business. No visibility means no calls. No calls means no business.
